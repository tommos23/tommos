Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science MComp

"Much that is central to computer science has been motivated by the development of artificial intelligence. The Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science degree course is designed to cover the fundamentals of both artificial intelligence and computer science." From the University artificial intelligence website

"A computer science degree is excellent preparation for your future career. It will sharpen your analytical skills, stretch your creative talents and provide you with practical skills that are relevant to the modern world.
Computer Science is our classic degree course that will provide you with a broad knowledge base in the diverse field of computing. You'll learn to understand the theoretical principles underlying a problem, and how to engineer a solution. You'll also become familiar with the practical issues involved in developing reliable, effective software systems in business or industry. As well as learning to program, and to think analytically, you'll be encouraged to work in teams and develop your communication skills." From the University computer science website
